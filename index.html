The Death Acre(Angola 🇦🇴)

The Death Acre (Doodsakker) is on the coast of Angola between Foz do Cunene and Tombua along the coast. This route is extremely dangerous because the waves crash against the dune face, with "experts" saying it's almost impossible to ride because the only way through is at low tide. But in our opinion thats what makes it fun isnt it?
